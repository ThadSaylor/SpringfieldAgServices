Improve Production

Springfield Ag Services exists to help farmers improve productions. We meet with farmers to discuss their plans for the upcoming crop year. We go over fertility issues, what crops will be planted where, and the varieties that would be best for each field.



Protect the Environment

After crops are planted, we scout their fields once per week and write recommendations for insect control, weed management, growth regulators, water control, and fertility needs. We do all of this in a way that protects our environment.







Support Local Business

Springfield Ag Services is a locally owned family business in Brownsville, Tennesee. This business consists of hard workers willing to do whatever it takes to satisfy our customers.



Choose Your Pricing

We take care of hauling and spreading or, if you want to haul it yourself, we will price just the spreading. We can do straight rate or VRA. We will help you select the best lime source for your situation.
