Family Origins

Kevin and Dale Springfield grew up together on a small farm in Mississippi. The brothers went to college at Mississippi State where they received B.S. degrees in Agricultural Business. In 1999, they moved to West Tennessee and started their business which is now known as Springfield Ag Services.






Business Information

The family business first started scouting cotton. Within a year, they began scouting wheat. By 2003, the business was scouting cotton, wheat, soybeans, and corn. Also in 2003, the business began to do soil sampling which has been very successful. Lime spreading has been a recent addition to the business.






Current Status

Recently, Springfield Ag Services has started a secondary business for trucking. Now in 2018, the business has been exceeding expectations. This small family business has been working harder than ever to become more successful.
